About Us

Who We Are

The Arty Bake is a local family-owned and operated bakery specializing in Swiss, French and German baked goods and pastries. The bakery has been in the same location in Moore Street for over 100 years. We use fine ingredients and traditional methods to produce exceptional breads, artisanal breads, ciabattas, pan loafs, pastries, gluten free (and other dietary needs) and of course....cookies, cakes and donuts!

Our Vision

While we continually strive for innovation, the same care and attention to detail just like we always have at Arty Bake, we are still making our creations the way our mentors made them, no added preservatives or additives. And they'd be pretty proud of that. Our goal is to provide all of our customers with an unparalleled product and experience. Our talented team of pastry chefs uses the purist of ingredients and cutting edge techniques to create a product that is truly superior in quality. We constantly provide outstanding customer service and believe that customer satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We are dedicated to provide our family of employees with a respectful and positive work environment where everyone is equal.
